# TurnoutLens — Civic Engagement Targeting Dashboard

## Goal
Build an interactive analytics tool to help civic engagement organizations benchmark county-level voter turnout and identify priority geographies for outreach based on historical participation trends.

## Key Questions
- Which counties consistently underperform on turnout relative to their state?
- Where has turnout declined or improved over recent election cycles?
- Which counties show the greatest potential for engagement impact?

## Users
- Civic engagement organizations
- Policy analysts
- Researchers
- Journalists

## Scope (v1)
- County-level turnout (2004–2022)
- Benchmarking by geography
- Interactive time-series comparisons
- Targeting-oriented summary metrics
- Methodology documentation

## Out of Scope (v1)
- Demographic breakdowns
- Predictive modeling
- Individual-level data
- Persuasion or partisan targeting
